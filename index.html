ArduTracker

The ArduTracker is a light weight, easy to build FPV Tracker. It uses the downlink of the audio channel from the Transmitter as telemetry link. But this project is different from most others. ArduTracker is a continuous 360° degree Tracker with a special hinge. This Video shows it best.

Special thanks to Cesco1 who made this possible.
